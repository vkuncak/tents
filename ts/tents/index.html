Tents: Recreation of Tents from Simon Tatham's Puzzles

Click on a cell to toggle its state. The goal is to place tents around trees without violating the rules:

  • The number of tents in each row and column must match the given totals.
  • It must be possible to match up tents and all trees trees in a one-to-one correspondence such that a tent and a corresponding tree are left, right, below, or above relative to each other.
  • A tent cannot have any other tents in the 8 fields surrounding it (up, down, left, right, and the four diagonal corners).

Notes:

  • Empty cells can be left blank or marked by x .
  • Clicking on a cell toggles its state between unmarked, marked, and tent.
  • If there are multiple solutions, the system will have chosen one particular solution, which you need to guess. Life is not fair!
  • To reproduce a specific puzzle, copy the puzzle number X and then use it in 
    ?id=X
    at the end of the url.
